I am an Instructional Designer by training with a proven expertise on data analyses, e.g., clustering play behaviors from a game (Rioja, 2024), comparing outcomes of two versions of a questionnaire (Rioja, 2023).

With five years’ experience as a research assistant in a cognitive neuroscience lab (Brain and Learning Lab), my tasks ranged from data management (design, collect, clean, analyse, communicate) to programming and launching apps (JS, PHP, Laravel, AWS EC2/S3, Azure).

While working for the Brain and Learning Lab, I successfully completed the ‘common core’ of the computer science school 42 Lausanne where I learned to program in C and C++. The acquired skills allowed me to develop a RESTful API in Python (Flask) to collect logs from a video game in another EdTech lab (Laboratoire d’Innovation Pédagogique).

In the past three years, I mentored groups of 3-to-5 students on acquiring soft skills and taught courses about R in an interactive manner which increased attendants’ self-reported digital knowledge by 28.7%.
